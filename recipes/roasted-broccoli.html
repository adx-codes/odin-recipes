Roasted broccoli

Description

Roasted broccoli is easy to make and so much more flavorsome than boiled. My favorite part is the roasted sliced stem pieces.

Ingredients

  • 14 ounces broccoli
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven
  2. Cut broccoli and mix with olive oil in a bowl
  3. Roast in the preheated oven
